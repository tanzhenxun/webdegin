Malaysia Culture
What is Malaysia Culture
Malaysia is a multi-cultural society. The main ethnic groups are the native Malays as well as large populations of Chinese, and Indians. When visiting the country it is clear that the ethnicities retain their religions, customs and way of life. The family is considered the centre of the social structure. As a result there is a great emphasis on unity, loyalty and respect for the elderly. The family is the place where the individual can be guaranteed both emotional and financial support.
Festivals often result in a public holiday, making Malaysia the only Southeast Asian country with the most number of public holidays.
